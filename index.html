What?

The Women in Tech Symposium is a signature event that fosters women's interest in technology and introduces them to the wonder of innovation in our daily lives. This is the first three-day all-Kerala summit organized by the Tinkerhub WIT collective and is open to all women. We create impact through action, to build and set women up for success.

Because when women rise, we all rise.



Why?

The core objective is to bring women from all across Kerala together to learn, network, and be inspired. We aim to provide opportunities for women from all walks of life to attend the tech conference through this programme.

We believe that this would be a stepping stone to helping them break the societal and gender barrier and dream high.


OUR THEME

Breaking The Curse of Perfection

The loss of self-confidence that comes with falling short of perfectionist expectations can push girls into a deep fear of failure preventing them from trying new things or taking risksLet's put an end to the perfectionist's curse. We never put pressure on you to achieve perfection; instead, here you can share anything, and seek mentorship.

We are here for you, and we accept you exactly the way you are.
